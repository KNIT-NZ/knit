Debate in the House of Representatives is highly constrained by procedure, and more experienced or legal-minded Members have a natural advantage in this context. All Members should be empowered to have the same leverage to understand and influence the course of proceedings during House Business. 

Our simple web application Open Order will rapidly parse natural language queries relating to the Standing Orders and Speakers’ Rulings for use in the House. Members will have access from any device to the century of procedural precedent that informs their experience, and allow them to be more effective and confident dealing with matters arising in real-time.

The target user base is small for this project, which serves as a proof of concept and a clear signal of our intention to build and ship tools that address specific opportunities in digital innovation without waiting to be asked.
