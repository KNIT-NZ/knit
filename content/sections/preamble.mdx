Democracy has not kept pace with the speed of modern life.<FootnoteRef id="1" />

The institutions that claim to represent the public often move too slowly, aggregate too crudely, and respond too weakly to the shape of lived reality.

KNIT exists to explore what comes next: not the abandonment of democracy, but its reconstruction.
